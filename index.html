yuppie

Coined in the early 1980s.

Meaning young urban professional.

Usually referring to one who exhibits pretentious or snobbish behavior.

Simialarly to the Hipster they're often found in coffee shops typing away for hours, wearing stylish black glasses, and of middle class.
